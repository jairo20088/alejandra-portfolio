What I do

User-Centered Design & Research

Conduct in-depth user research and usability testing to identify user needs and pain points. Utilize insights to develop personas, journey maps, and data-driven design solutions that enhance user engagement and satisfaction.

Prototyping & Interaction Design

Create low-fidelity and high-fidelity wireframes and interactive prototypes using tools like Figma. Develop intuitive, user-friendly interfaces that align with business objectives and usability standards.

AI-Driven Solutions & Data Analysis

Design and implement AI-driven tools and solutions, leveraging data analysis and machine learning to optimize user experiences. Continuously iterate on designs based on user feedback and emerging technologies.

Who I am

UX Designer based out of Columbia, MO

I’m a graduate student at DePaul University pursuing a Master’s degree in Human-Computer Interaction. I’m passionate about bringing accessibility to digital interfaces. My current focus is on Generative AI, exploring how it can be leveraged to enhance user experiences and drive innovation in design.

My Mission Statement

My mission is to design inclusive and accessible user experiences that empower all individuals, regardless of their abilities or backgrounds. I believe that well-crafted, functional, and user-friendly designs can profoundly impact people’s lives, making technology more accessible and enriching their experiences. Rooted in my personal and professional experiences, I am committed to creating designs that prioritize accessibility and functionality, ensuring that every user feels included and can interact with products effortlessly. I am dedicated to upholding the principles of Human-Centered Design, where aesthetics enhance usability, and accessibility is never compromised. My goal is to contribute to a world where design serves everyone, making technology an ally rather than an obstacle.
